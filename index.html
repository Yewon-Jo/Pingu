Pingu is a British-Swiss stop-motion clay animated children's comedy television series created by Otmar Gutmann and produced from 1986 to 2000 for Swiss television, and from 2003 to 2006 for British television, by The Pygos Group (formerly Trickfilmstudio and later Pingu Filmstudio).
